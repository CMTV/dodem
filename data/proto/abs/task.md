$$ |f| \geq f $$

---

2.

$$ |f|^2 = f^2 $$

---

3.

$$ \sqrt{f^2} = |f| $$

---

4.

$$ |f\cdot g| = |f||g| $$

Следствие:

$$ |f|^n = |f^n| $$

---

5.

$$ |f-g| = |g-f| $$

---

6.

$$ |f| - |g| \leq |f\pm g| \leq |f| + |g| $$
