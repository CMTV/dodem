1.

$$ \sqrt{f} < g \Leftrightarrow \andcases{ f \geq 0 \\ g > 0 \\ g^2 > f } $$

2.

$$ \sqrt{f} > g \Leftrightarrow \orcases{ \andcases{g \geq 0 \\ f > g^2} \\[15px] \andcases{ f \geq 0 \\ g < 0} } $$

3.

$$ \sqrt{f} < \sqrt{g} \Leftrightarrow \andcases{ f \geq 0 \\ g > f } $$
